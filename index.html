The Benthic Environment

The Benthic Environment is the ocean floor and the space around it.
As the name suggests, this environment is home to the benthos, (organisms that live close to the ocean floor.)
This environment is divided into five zones.

Intertidal Zone

<200m below sea level, very shallow

It's not very deep.

Sublittoral Zone

up to 200m below sea level
